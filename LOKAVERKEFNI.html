About me

Made in Iceland


My name is Arna Lea
I am 23 years old, and I come from Patreksfjörður, a small town in the Westfjords of Iceland.

While growing up I was surrounded by nature and open landscape, and time moved slow. That gave me opportunities to explore the environment around me and it helped me become independent. I discovered early that I love art and design. I was an artistic, theatrical kid and I developed a desire to shape my surroundings, make a change and create.
